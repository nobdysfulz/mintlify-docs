# Trends

The Trends page gives you interactive charts to analyze your team's performance patterns over time. Use it to spot growth, identify slow periods, and make data-driven decisions about where to focus.

## Three Views

### Production

Shows your team's closed production month by month:

- **Bar chart** -- Units closed and GCI side by side for each month
- **Line chart** -- Total sales volume as a trend line over the year

### Financials

Overlays three financial metrics on a single bar chart:

- **GCI** -- Gross commission income earned
- **Expenses** -- Operating costs for the month
- **Net Profit** -- What remains after expenses

### Lead Gen

Shows lead generation activity as trend lines over time:

- **Contacts** -- Total prospecting contacts made each month
- **Listing Appts** -- Listing consultations held
- **Buyer Appts** -- Buyer consultations held

Compare this chart against your Production chart. You should see lead gen activity reflected in closings roughly 30 to 90 days later.

## Filtering by Agent

Team leaders can view trends for the entire team or drill down to a single agent.

## Using Trends for Planning

- Use the Production chart to identify your historically slow months and plan campaigns to offset the dip
- Use the Financials chart to confirm that revenue growth outpaces expense growth
- Use the Lead Gen chart to hold agents accountable. Consistent inputs produce consistent outputs
