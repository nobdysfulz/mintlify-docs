# Leaderboard

The Leaderboard ranks every agent on your team across key production and activity metrics. It is a motivational tool and a quick diagnostic for where the team stands.

## Leaderboard Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures |
|--------|------------------|
| **GCI** | Gross commission income from closed transactions |
| **Units** | Number of closed transactions |
| **Contacts** | Total prospecting contacts from daily lead gen logs |
| **Listing Appts Held** | Listing consultations that took place |
| **Buyer Appts Held** | Buyer consultations that took place |
| **Written Offers** | Offers submitted |

## Time Frames

- **This Month** -- Current calendar month only
- **YTD** -- January 1 through today
- **This Year** -- Full calendar year

## Podium Display

The top 3 agents are shown on a podium at the top of the page. First place is in the center and slightly elevated. Great for sharing on a team monitor or in team meetings.

## Full Rankings Table

Below the podium, every agent is listed in ranked order with their numbers for the selected metric.

## Office Filter

Office admins and franchise admins can filter the Leaderboard by office.

## Mini Leaderboard on the Dashboard

The Dashboard always shows a mini Leaderboard with the top 5 agents by GCI. Click through to the full Leaderboard for more detail.
