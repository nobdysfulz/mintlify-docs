# Integrations

The Integrations tab in Settings is where you connect Voru to other platforms in your workflow.

## Accessing Integrations

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Integrations** tab

## Setting Up an Integration

Click on any available integration to begin the setup process. Each integration has its own authorization flow -- you may be redirected to the external platform to grant access. Once connected, the integration status updates to show it is active.

## Disconnecting an Integration

To disconnect an integration, click its settings and select the disconnect option. Disconnecting stops data from syncing between Voru and that platform. Existing data in Voru is not deleted.

## Integration Questions

If you have questions about which integrations are available or need help setting one up, contact your team leader or the Voru support team.
