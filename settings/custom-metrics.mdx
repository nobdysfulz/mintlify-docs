# Custom Metrics

Custom Metrics let team leaders define up to 5 additional lead generation fields that agents log alongside the standard daily activity fields.

## Who Can Manage Custom Metrics

Only team leaders and admins can create, edit, or delete custom metrics.

## Setting Up Custom Metrics

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Custom Metrics** tab
3. Click **Add Custom Metric**
4. Enter a name for the metric
5. Save

Repeat for each additional metric (up to 5 total).

## Examples of Custom Metrics

- Video messages sent
- Social media posts published
- Door knocks
- Mailers sent
- Referrals asked for
- Follow-up emails sent
- Events attended

## Where Custom Metrics Appear

Once created, custom metrics appear in the Daily Lead Gen entry form, the Scorecards view, and Contest point assignments.

## Limit of 5 Custom Metrics

Choose the ones that matter most to your team's prospecting strategy and stick with them. Consistency in what you measure makes trend data meaningful over time.
