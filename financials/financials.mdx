# Financial Statement

The Financials page gives team leaders a complete picture of the business's financial health. It combines commission income from closed transactions with manually entered expenses to build a real profit and loss view.

## Commission Summary

At the top of the Financials page, each agent's commission income is summarized. This pulls directly from closed transactions in the Pipeline, so keeping your pipeline up to date is essential for accurate financial reporting.

## Monthly P&L Table

| Line Item | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **GCI** | Gross commission income from closed deals |
| **Cost of Sales** | Agent commission splits and referral fees |
| **Gross Profit** | GCI minus cost of sales |
| **Expenses** | Total operating expenses for the month |
| **Net Profit** | Gross profit minus expenses |

## Adding Financial Entries

Click **Add Entry** and fill in:

- **Category** -- The expense category
- **Description** -- A short note about what this expense was
- **Date** -- When the expense occurred
- **Actual Amount** -- What was actually spent
- **Budget Amount** -- What was budgeted for this expense

The difference between actual and budget is shown as variance.

## Expense Categories

Default categories: Lead Generation, Occupancy, Technology, Supplies, Education, Equipment, Other. Custom categories can be added to match your business structure.

## Who Can See Financials

Financial data is visible only to team leaders, office admins, and franchise admins. Individual agents do not have access to team financial reports.
