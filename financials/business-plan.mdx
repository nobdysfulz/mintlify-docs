# Business Plan

The Business Plan tool helps you translate your annual goals into a concrete monthly production forecast. Fill in your targets and assumptions once, and Voru builds a full 12-month projection automatically.

## Production Goals

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **GCI Goal** | Your total gross commission income target for the year |
| **Units Goal** | Total transactions you plan to close |
| **Avg Sale Price (Listings)** | Average price of homes you plan to list |
| **Avg Sale Price (Buyers)** | Average price of homes your buyers plan to purchase |
| **Listing Commission Rate** | Your commission percentage on listing transactions |
| **Buyer Commission Rate** | Your commission percentage on buyer transactions |

## Conversion Rate Assumptions

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Appt-to-Taken Rate** | What percentage of appointments result in a signed agreement |
| **Taken-to-Close Rate** | What percentage of signed agreements result in a closed deal |
| **Contacts per Appointment** | How many contacts it typically takes to set one appointment |

## Annual Budget

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Lead Generation** | Online leads, direct mail, ads |
| **Occupancy** | Office rent, utilities |
| **Technology** | CRM, tools, software subscriptions |
| **Supplies** | Printing, signs, office supplies |
| **Education** | Coaching, training, conferences |
| **Equipment** | Computers, cameras, lockboxes |
| **Other** | Anything that does not fit above |

## Monthly Projection Table

Once your inputs are complete, Voru generates a 12-month projection table showing expected units, GCI, lead gen activity needed, and budgeted expenses per month. Projections use a seasonality model so monthly numbers reflect real market patterns.

## Updating Your Business Plan

Revisit your business plan quarterly. If your conversion rates improve or your average sale price shifts, update the inputs and the projections recalculate instantly.
