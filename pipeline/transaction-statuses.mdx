# Transaction Statuses Explained

Every transaction in Voru moves through a series of statuses that reflect where it stands in the process. Keeping statuses current ensures your pipeline is accurate and your reporting is reliable.

## Status Definitions

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Pre-Signed** | A listing or buyer agreement has been discussed but not yet signed. Use this for deals in negotiation or on deck. |
| **Active** | The listing is live on the market, or the buyer is actively searching. |
| **Pending** | A contract has been signed and the deal is moving toward closing. |
| **Closed** | The transaction has successfully closed. This is when GCI is counted in your financials and leaderboard. |
| **x-Cancelled** | The deal was cancelled before closing. Prefixed with x so it sorts separately and does not affect active metrics. |
| **y-Sale Failed** | The transaction failed after going under contract. Prefixed with y for the same reason. |

## How Statuses Affect Reporting

- **Closed** transactions count toward your YTD GCI, Units, and Volume on the Dashboard and Leaderboard
- **Pending** transactions appear in Pending GCI on the Dashboard KPI card and in Upcoming Closings
- **Active** and **Pre-Signed** transactions appear in the pipeline funnel but do not count toward closed production
- **x-Cancelled** and **y-Sale Failed** transactions are preserved for your records but excluded from performance metrics

## Listing vs. Buyer Transactions

Each transaction has a **Type**:

- **Listing** -- You represent the seller
- **Buyer** -- You represent the buyer

Both types move through the same statuses and both count toward GCI and units when closed.

## Days on Market (DOM)

For Listing transactions, Voru calculates DOM automatically from the list date.

## Days to Close

For Pending transactions, Voru tracks days from when the deal went pending to the expected close date.
