# Welcome to Voru

Voru is a real estate leadership platform built to help team leaders and agents track performance, manage their pipeline, and grow with purpose. Whether you run a small team or a multi-office operation, Voru gives you the visibility and tools to lead effectively.

## What Voru Does

Voru brings together every critical part of your business into one place:

- **Pipeline Management** -- Track every transaction from first contact to closed deal
- **Daily Lead Generation** -- Log activity each day so nothing falls through the cracks
- **Goals and Scorecards** -- Set annual targets and measure progress month by month
- **Leaderboard** -- See who is leading and who needs support, at a glance
- **Financial Tracking** -- Understand your income, expenses, and profitability
- **Business Planning** -- Build your annual plan and project monthly production
- **Contests** -- Run team competitions to drive motivation and results
- **Nurture CRM** -- Stay in touch with future clients before they are ready to transact
- **Reports and Trends** -- Analyze patterns and print professional summaries
- **ROI Tracker** -- Know exactly which lead sources are worth your investment

## Who Uses Voru

Voru is designed for real estate teams of all sizes:

| Role | What They Use Voru For |
|------|------------------------|
| **Agent** | Log daily activity, track personal pipeline, monitor goals |
| **Team Leader** | Oversee all agents, run contests, review scorecards, manage financials |
| **Office Admin** | Manage a single office's agents and reporting |
| **Franchise Admin** | View and manage multiple offices across an entire franchise |
