Whether you're documenting internal processes, writing customer-facing FAQs, or publishing product guides, Knowledgebase gives you the tools to create structured, discoverable content that scales with your organization.

## How It Works

Going from zero to a live help center takes just a few steps. Here's the big picture before you dive in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a workspace">
    A workspace is your team's private hub inside Knowledgebase. Sign up at [app.knowledgebase.io](https://app.knowledgebase.io), choose a name and a URL slug, and invite your teammates to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write and edit articles">
    Use the built-in rich-text editor to write articles. Add headings, images, code blocks, callouts, and more — no markdown experience required, though markdown shortcuts are fully supported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize into collections">
    Group related articles into collections to give your help center clear, navigable structure. Collections can be nested, reordered, and color-coded so readers always know where they are.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish to your help center">
    Flip your workspace's visibility to **Public** and share the URL with your customers. Optionally map a custom domain so the help center lives at `help.yourcompany.com`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
