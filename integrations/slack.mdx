The Knowledgebase Slack integration puts your entire help center one slash command away. Instead of switching context to look something up, your team can search for articles, preview results, and share formatted article cards right inside any Slack channel or direct message — keeping conversations moving and answers easy to find.

## Installing the Slack App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Settings and navigate to Slack">
    In Knowledgebase, click your workspace name in the top-left corner and select **Workspace Settings**. Go to **Integrations → Slack**, then click the **Install** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Knowledgebase in your Slack workspace">
    You'll be redirected to Slack's OAuth authorization page. Review the permissions requested — Knowledgebase needs the ability to read channel information and post messages on your behalf — then click **Allow**. You'll be brought back to Knowledgebase once authorization is complete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first search with /kb search">
    Open any Slack channel or direct message and type `/kb search` followed by your query, for example `/kb search password reset`. Knowledgebase will return a list of matching articles with titles and short excerpts directly in the channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share an article with your team">
    In the search results, click the **Share** button next to any article. Knowledgebase will post a formatted article card to the channel — including the article title, a short description, and a direct link — so anyone in the conversation can click through to read it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  In some Slack workspaces, installing third-party apps requires approval from a Slack workspace admin. If you don't see the authorization screen after clicking **Install**, ask your Slack admin to approve the Knowledgebase app under **Slack Admin → Manage Apps**.
</Note>

## Available Slash Commands

Once the app is installed, the following slash commands are available to everyone in your Slack workspace:

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/kb search [query]` | Searches your Knowledgebase articles and returns the top matching results. Replace `[query]` with any search term, e.g. `/kb search billing invoice`. |
| `/kb latest` | Returns the 5 most recently published articles in your help center, so your team always knows what's new. |
| `/kb share [article-url]` | Posts a formatted article card to the current channel. Paste any article URL from your help center to generate a rich preview with the title and description. |

## Article Publish Notifications

You can configure Knowledgebase to automatically post a notification in a Slack channel whenever a new article is published. This is useful for keeping your support team and customer-facing staff up to date whenever new documentation goes live.

To set it up, go to **Workspace Settings → Integrations → Slack → Notifications Channel**, then select the Slack channel where you'd like notifications to appear. From that point on, every newly published article will trigger a message in that channel with the article title and a direct link.

<Tip>
  For the fastest access to article search, add `/kb search` to your Slack toolbar shortcuts. In Slack, click the **lightning bolt ⚡ icon** in the message composer, find `/kb search`, and select **Add to shortcuts**. You'll be able to launch a knowledge search with a single click from any channel.
</Tip>
